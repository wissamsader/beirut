The man himself

Khalil cooks. Kholyo sings. Same person.

Foul Kholyo is an old-school morning house on the Ouzai road with a couple of tables, a counter, and the funniest natural person in the Lebanese restaurant business behind it. Khalil — Kholyo — simmers foul, stacks hummus with meat or shrimp, and sings while he does it. Mornings only; joy guaranteed.

Reviewers come for the food and return for the man — "the funniest natural person" running the warmest little room in Ouzai.

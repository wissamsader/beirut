The story

FIFTY-EIGHT YEARS
OF CHICKEN DONE RIGHT

Lala has been grilling chicken for Beirut since 1967 — three generations of one family name over the door. Today the room is small and modern, the spice rub is unchanged, and the grilled potatoes with garlic have their own fan club. Order the chicken, add the garlic, thank the year 1967.

The roots

IT STARTED AS ROBERT LALA, A NAME THE CITY TRUSTED

Before the new counter and the modern room, there was the original Robert Lala grilled chicken — Achrafieh, Sassine, Furn El Chebbak. The family name never left the sign, and the recipe never left the family.

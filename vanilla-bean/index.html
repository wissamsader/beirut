Baked in front of you

At Vanilla Bean the kitchen has no door: you watch the owner and her mother roll croissants and build cakes a few steps from your table. The name is literal — real vanilla beans go into the signature bakes — and the za'atar croissant has quietly become a Hamra-district legend. Seats are few; mornings are busiest; the coffee wall is short and correct.

Since 1965, the king holds court

Three generations of AUB students have eaten at Malek al Foul, and the menu still fits on one card: foul, hummus in every direction, balila, fatteh, eggs. Bassam Badran — Abu Hadi — runs the small Hamra counter the way it's run since 1965: huge ladles, quick service, most of it carried out the door in paper bags.

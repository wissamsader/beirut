SCENE ONE — THE SEA

ext. corniche, golden hour

On the Avenue de Paris, where the Corniche meets the sea, Kahwat Ras Beirut serves Lebanon the way it tastes in memories — mezze on cool marble, soujouk with pomegranate, oriental nights with live music, and coffee that lasts until 2 AM. A 4.8-star love letter to the city.

ORIENTAL NIGHTS

When the sun drops behind the lighthouse, the oud comes out. Live tarab evenings — the old songs, sung the old way, with the windows open to the sea. Coffee turns to arak, and nobody checks the time.

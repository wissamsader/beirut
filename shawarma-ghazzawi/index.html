Two counters. One family.

Ghazzawi runs two counters side by side in Aisha Bakkar: the shawarma shop where the spits turn in the window, and the sister falafel shop next door. It's one of the oldest shawarma addresses in the area — Gaza-style, carved in front of you, managed by Abu Firas and his sons with the patience of people who've done this their whole lives.

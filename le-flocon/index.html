The atelier

Ice cream, treated like haute pâtisserie

Le Flocon — “the snowflake” — is Sodeco's white-awninged atelier of frozen things: gelato churned in-house, sorbets shaped into the fruits they came from, pistachio domes plated like patisserie, and a salted caramel whose reputation precedes it by several streets. Un voyage de saveurs, ten to ten, daily.

Behind the glass, the team sculpts fruit-shaped pieces and builds teddy-bear cones for the brave. Everything is churned on site, in small batches, on a voyage de saveurs.

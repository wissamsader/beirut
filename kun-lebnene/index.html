The house

Elegance with a Lebanese accent

Kun Lebnéné — “Be Lebanese” — is both the name and the house philosophy. The room is elegant and luxurious, the welcome is warm from the first moment, and the kitchen treats the Lebanese repertoire like an inheritance: fattoush bright as a garden, hummus like silk, warak enab folded tight as letters home.

In summer the name travels — a seasonal address in Batroun at Capo — but the heart stays here, by the sea in Beirut, where the room glows and the kitchen keeps its promises.

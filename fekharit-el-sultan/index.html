The evenings

When the oud starts, nobody leaves

Fekharit el Sultan serves Lebanon in earthenware: hummus in its variations, sfiha from the fern, octopus tender enough to make the reviews emotional — and on music nights, an oud and a voice that hold the whole room still. The owner hosts like the title is real, and by dessert you believe it.

On music nights the room turns into a small tarab theatre — an oud player, a singer, and tables that forget the time. Reserve ahead; the sultan's court fills fast.

The programLive oud & classical Arabic song evenings from 9 PM — announced on the door and by phone. Call to catch the next one.

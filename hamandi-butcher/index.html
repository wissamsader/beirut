From the case to the coals

Hamandi is a butcher shop that grills. You point at the case — tawouk, kafta, lamb cubes, the day's skewers — and it goes straight from the marble to the charcoal to your sidewalk table. Tiny, humble and honest, with meat reviewers call the best-tasting kebab in Beirut. Most of Hamra orders it delivered; the smart ones eat it ten steps from the fire.

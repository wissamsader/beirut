Baytouti means 'homestyle', and that's the whole idea: foul, fatteh and msabaha cooked the old way in Haret Hreik — then plated like nobody's business. Crowns of crisp bread, rings of cashews, pink pickles fanned like petals. A perfect 5.0 rating, mornings only, closed Mondays. Come hungry, leave photographing your plate.

MEET THE MAN

Hakim cooks. Hakim serves. Hakim remembers your order.

Hakim Beirut is a one-man show in Ras Beirut: the owner himself carving the spits, working the saj, stacking the burgers and telling the stories — eighteen hours a day. Every review is five stars, every plate comes with a welcome, and the lights stay on long after the rest of the street sleeps.

From 8 in the morning to 2 after midnight, the owner himself carves the spit, works the saj, flips the burgers and pours the coffee — with a story for everyone who waits.

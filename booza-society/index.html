The maker

One owner. One machine.
Real fruit.

Booza Society is a one-man institution on Jeanne d'Arc street: the owner in his chef's coat, a churning machine, and fruit bought like an argument with the season. Little sugar, natural everything, and flavors that appear and vanish with the market — plus oriental classics like sahlab & miskeh that taste like childhood.

Little sugar, natural ingredients, and flavors that change with the season — when strawberries end, the strawberry ends. Nobody argues.

The two friends

Dinner at their house, essentially

Kelly's Fish Lounge is run by two friends, Elias and Kelly, and it shows: the fish lies on ice by the kitchen, the chalkboard prices change with the boats, and the whole place feels like being welcomed into their home — warm, effortless, and always ending with one more story and one more plate.

The catch lies on ice by the kitchen — you point, they cook. Whole fish, ceviche, octopus with pomegranate, and whatever the sea offered Beirut this morning.
